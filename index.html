Cryogenic attenuator

Quantum Computing Cryogenic Attenuators feature excellent thermal conductivity and high stability at extremely low temperatures (<100 mK). On one hand, they can function as signal amplitude attenuators with options such as 0 dB, 5 dB, 20 dB, and 10 dB. On the other hand, they can serve as heat sinks for efficient cooling transfer. These attenuators are suitable for applications in deep‑space exploration, radio astronomy, quantum computing, and wireless communications. The Model LM‑AT‑8G‑*‑C Quantum Computing Cryogenic Attenuator operates in the 4 K temperature range and is characterized by outstanding thermal conductivity and high temperature stability.
